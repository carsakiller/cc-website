Default Roles
Role Name Description
Owner The owner of the server. This role is the most protected. These players have ultimate control.
Supervisor Players with this role will be notified when important events happen.
Admin Admins are like moderators but they have more access to game modifying commands. These players are extremely trustworthy.
Moderator Moderators are able to kick and ban players. These players are very trustworthy.
Auth Authorized players are trusted to spawn vehicles and not cause too much damage.
Everyone Every player is given this role. This role has access to some general and mostly harmless commands.
